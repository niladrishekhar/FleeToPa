Taptapani Tour
Taptapani is situated at 67 km from Gopalpur. Taptapani offers an exclusive action packed tour coupled with forest, hot water spring. Taptapani is also the home of primitive Kutia Kondh tribe, the Dingaria Kondh tribe, Bonda and the Mali tribes.

Taptapani is a bubbling hot sulphur spring located about 50 km from Berhampur in Ganjam District. It is surrounded by hills inhabited by the Orissan tribal. The sulphur present in the water is said to cure skin ailments. The water extremely hot near the origin is canalized into a pond where people take their dip.

The archaism is Orissa is retained by her tribal folks. They continue to dwell in remote areas od the deep forest and hilly interiors. The residents who are expert hunters, independent and worshipper of numerous gods and goddess attracts sociologist and tourists all the year round.
Stay
The Orissa Tourism Development Corporation has pipelines to transport the water from the spring to Panthanivas, which is then available inside the bathroom. So enjoy healing powers of the hot sulphur spring in the privacy of your own room.
