Rambha Tour
A small picturesque travel destination on the Chilka lake in the state of Orissa, India, Rambha is one amongst the tourist destination for Chilika which is apparently having a clam and quite environment and surroundings. Rambha once was a princely estate of Khalikote and the king has a palace there

Other then the Dolphins and migratory birds also enjoy the scenic beauty from one of the many islands present there like the Breakfast islad, Bird island, Honeymoon and the Sanakuda island and the other caves present there.

Just by the side of the water with hills in the horizon enjoy your stay here with the safe and comfortable Panthanivas present over here
