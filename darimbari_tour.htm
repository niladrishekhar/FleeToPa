Daringbadi Tour
Dubbed as the Kashmir of Orissa – Daringbadi the only hill station in Orissa is a place you will fall in love at first sight. At 3000ft the plateau tucked away in the Eastern Ghats welcomes its tourist with cool breeze, twittering of birds and the wilderness of the world.

For traditional tourist there are sight scenes like hill viewpoint and coffee garden and both are worth a visit. The Kandha Women here wear tattoos in their face and huge metal earring. The pine, Sal, eucalyptus, plantations will give you a feel of Mother Nature. The valley will surely win your heart with its tranquil beauty.
