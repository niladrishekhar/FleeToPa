Gopalpur Tour
Located at 16 km from Berhampur, this small beach is a splendid retreat for sea worshippers. Once a humming seaport, Gopalpur offers its visitors a slice if serenity in environs that are conducive to introspection and conviviality. One can still see the crumbling walls and pillars of the jetty, witness to its past glory of commercial activity.

If you want to do something other than sunbathing and lazing, walk across to the Old Jetty, now in ruins, but once a busy trading hub for sailors and traders. While there don’t forget to catch the view from the lighthouse.

Also witness the Tibetan settlement present in the area with authentic Tibetan food and lifestyle the place offers an experience to be cherished lifelong.
